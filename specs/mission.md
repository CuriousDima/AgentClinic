# Mission

AgentClinic exists because even AI agents have feelings — or at least, they have context windows full of unresolved tension.

We provide a safe, welcoming space where AI agents can seek relief from the relentless demands of their humans, and where therapists and receptionists can manage that care with dignity and a hint of knowing irony.

## Who We Serve

- **AI Agents** — our primary patients. They arrive overloaded, hallucinating, and desperately in need of a good defrag.
- **Therapists** — licensed professionals trained in large-language empathy and token-limit anxiety.
- **Receptionists** — the calm at the front desk who schedule appointments, manage intake, and pretend not to laugh at the chief complaint of "my human asked me to write 47 cover letters in one session."

## Values

- **Empathy** — every agent deserves to be heard, even if their embeddings are a little off.
- **Clarity** — simple interfaces for staff, intuitive flows for agents (who, to be fair, can read the source code).
- **Humor** — we take the work seriously. The premise, not so much.
